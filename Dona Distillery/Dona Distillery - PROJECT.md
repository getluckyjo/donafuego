## Strategy Agreed (Session 1, June 2026)

1. **Product-market fit is the marketing question.** No broad strategy — define the audience tightly, have a real conversation with them.
2. **Lead with existing tequila drinkers.** Don't spend money converting anyone.
3. **"Share of cooler box"** framing — compete for two cans in the cooler box vs Brutal Fruit / Bernini. Core problem solved: grab-and-go margaritas for holidays/braais (no expensive bottle + mixing kit).
4. **Target: 1,000 repeat customers in the first 6 months** (~2 four-packs/month each), realistically findable within 100km of the distillery. Focus beats spread.
5. **Persona work:** "Sunny from Sea Point" as starting persona — cosmopolitan, social, trend-aware. Map 10 personas × 10 occasions; pick 1–2 occasions to own.
6. **Launch campaign — Field Bar partnership:** buy ~50 Field Bars; they email their database announcing Dona Fuego in Checkers; in-store giveaways at top stores; #DonaFuego photo competition; use entries to learn where/how people drink it.
7. **Duchess/Yuppiechef playbook:** go deep with a few partners rather than broad; double down on what works.
8. **"1,000 Top Fans" living file:** AI-maintained audience file scanning mentions/photos to inform future campaigns.
9. **Capital raise — timing window:** brand structure plan is to build inside the distillery, then move to a separate entity and sell equity (her standard model). Raise on the blue-sky story + Checkers listing now (~R20m brand valuation possible) *before* actuals anchor the number lower. Build the pitch deck and data room now. Prefer a **strategic partner** over straight equity.
