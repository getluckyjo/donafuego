The 60-second pitch

South Africans drink more RTDs than almost anyone on earth — it's the world's third-largest ready-to-drink market. But nearly all of it is vodka-based. Tequila, the fastest-growing spirit on the planet, barely has a can on the shelf.

Doña Fuego fills that gap. Real Mexican tequila, real juice, 7% ABV, in a 250ml can. Made by Doña Distillery — the Cape Town distillery that already imports bulk tequila at scale and quietly supplies the tequila inside competing RTD brands.

Checkers agreed. After multiple tasting panels, SA's fastest-growing retailer listed Doña Fuego exclusively in 300 stores nationwide — an opening order of roughly R3 million, the biggest purchase order in the distillery's history.

The investment covers the whole tequila business — the Doña Fuego cans, the Fuego Tequila bottled range already selling nationwide, and the bulk tequila supply arm. Projected R21m revenue and ~28% EBITDA margins by December 2027.

Why Checkers matters

Checkers is South Africa's fastest-growing grocer five years running — a near-R100 billion brand growing sales at 13.8% a year, with 311 LiquorShops and Sixty60 delivery up 47.7% to R18.9bn. A 300-store exclusive covers essentially its entire premium footprint.

Checkers has a long playbook of exclusive premium liquor listings that pull feet through the door. Doña Fuego is the tequila chapter — positioned at the premium end of the shelf (R49 a can against R39–42 mainstream), justified by real tequila, real juice and higher ABV.

The founder

Danielle Schoeman

South Africa's youngest female master distiller — that's Food & Home's headline, not ours. Stellenbosch business degree, six years in the corporate world, then she traded heels for tekkies, moved to the Karoo and taught herself to make tequila after tasting the real thing in Mexico.

In 2019 she founded Doña Distillery in Cape Town — 100% female-owned, built around custom vacuum stills, the only ones in Africa. By year two the distillery had taken national trophies for Best Ready-to-Drink and Best Low/No-Alcohol Drink, plus a string of international medals.

Today Doña produces its own portfolio (Mahala Botanical, Toor Whisky, Fuego Tequila and Doña Fuego), contract-distills for other brands, exports to Europe and North America — and imports the bulk Mexican tequila that powers a growing share of South Africa's RTD shelf.
